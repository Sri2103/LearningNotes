## RUIN CHECK

- R — Failure risk increased? YES / NO
- U — Updated failure probability high? YES / NO
- I — Irreversible damage possible? YES / NO
- N — No-go threshold crossed? YES / NO

## STOP CHECK

- S — New warning signal?
- T — Tail risk severe?
- O — Safer options exist?
- P — Pull out now?

If ≥ 2 YES → ABORT
